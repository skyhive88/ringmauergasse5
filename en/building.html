Construction & Materials

Solid construction with thoughtful details

A solid structure in reinforced concrete and limestone block, with double-glazed insulated windows and heating billed by actual consumption.

The gas condensing heating system measures heat consumption individually per unit — no flat rate, billed by actual use. The kitchen features granite worktops, a stainless-steel sink, and a ceramic glass cooktop; flooring is parquet in living and sleeping areas, ceramic tile in wet rooms.

"Adlergarten" Community
