Location

Old town Rheinfelden, on the Rhine

Rheinfelden is a former Zähringer town on the Rhine, directly on the German border — its historic old town alive with events all year round.

Fasnacht (carnival), Mode-Mai, the national holiday, Usestuhlete, a weekly farmers' market, and various art exhibitions shape the calendar in the old town. The lakeside Strandbad, the SoleUno spa resort, and the ice rink are all close by, offering variety for all ages. More information: www.rheinfelden.ch

Getting around

On foot, by bike, by public transport

Centrally located, everything is reachable within 8 to 15 minutes on foot — Migros, Coop, the post office, banks, schools, and the crossing into Germany.
