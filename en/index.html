Old Meets New

A home with history next door

Just beyond the balcony door begins the centuries-old town wall of Rheinfelden — stone and history, immediately adjacent to bright, quiet living space.

The maisonette sits secluded in a former spa garden, in the middle of the old town and yet private and quiet. Tall ceilings, abundant daylight, and a south-facing balcony with views across the rooftops define the roughly 141 m² of living space.
